Strategy
1

Custom Audience upload

We've already uploaded the 1,106 emails + phone numbers as a Meta Custom Audience. Meta matches these to real Facebook/Instagram profiles for precise targeting. Done.

2

Lookalike Audience (1%)

From those 1,106 profiles, Meta finds people with similar demographics, interests, and behaviors. This gives us a fresh acquisition pool of high-quality prospects. Done.

3

Win-Back ads (A/B test)

We'll run two ad formats simultaneously — a static image and a video testimonial — splitting the budget 50/50. Meta auto-optimizes delivery toward whichever converts better.

4

Native lead form

Users sign up directly inside Facebook/Instagram with a pre-filled form (1 tap). No friction, no redirect. Our data shows native forms convert 2x better than website traffic ($12.67 vs $26.03 per lead).
